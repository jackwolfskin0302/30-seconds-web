Site preference cookies

In order to provide you with a great experience on this site we provide the functionality to set your preferences for how this site runs when you use it. In order to remember your preferences we need to set cookies so that this information can be called whenever you interact with a page is affected by your preferences. These first party cookies are considered essential and therefore cannot be disabled, however they are not shared with any third parties.

Third party cookies

In some special cases we might also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section details which third party cookies you might encounter through this site. In any case, we will not use any third party cookies without your explicit consent.

Google Analytics cookies

This site uses Google Analytics which is one of the most widespread and trusted analytics solutions on the web in order to help us understand how you use the site and ways that we can improve your experience. While Google Analytics usually requires cookies in order to operate correctly, we have opted out of this requirement, in order to minimize tracking by third parties. We store some information for the duration of each visit instead, allowing us to track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit so we can continue to produce engaging content. This information is not stored after you leave the site, therefore not providing any uniquely identifiable information in the future or sharing any such information with third parties.

For more information on Google Analytics and cookies related to it, see the official Google Analytics page.
